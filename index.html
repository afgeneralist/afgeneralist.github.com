Things I've learned to do well

A by-product of my university hopping, injury enduring, globe-trotting and geeky lifestyle, I've learned a few things along the way:

  • My travels, both domestic and abroad, my IT position within a bio-tech company and my academic focus have given me a diverse set of experiences that give me the tools to communicate effectively regardless of the context.

Moving to Spain, hitch-hiking and couch-surfing, quitting University and my job to try and play professional soccer and electing for a pubic bone surgery that may not have healed have been the toughest but the most important decisions in my life.

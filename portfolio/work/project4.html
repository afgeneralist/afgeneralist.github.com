Custom registration system for non-profit organization's annual music contest

I've been the webmaster for San Francisco Bay Area non-profit SDMT for over a decade. As I've learned more about web development, I've improved upon the website - taking it from a strictly informational website in the 1990s to a completely online registration system today. Photo and video from SDMT's annual music contest are all online, and a data system is in place for tracking participants' achievements over the years.

The website uses WordPress for content management. I wrote a custom theme and registration system in PHP. A transition to a more efficient Ruby on Rails system is underway.
